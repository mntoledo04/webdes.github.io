Welcome

Welcome humans! We've been waiting for you. Do you hear it? That soft, haunting melody weaving through the waves, calling your name? You've followed it this far — beneath the crashing tides and into the shadowy depths. Don’t be afraid. We’ve lured you here for a reason. The ocean holds secrets older than time, whispered through salt and silence. Here, beneath the surface, the line between beauty and danger blurs. We are the voices you’ve heard in your dreams, the glinting eyes beneath the moonlit waves. Some call us sirens, others call us monsters — but you… you may call us home. Stay a while. Let the current pull you deeper.
